The Philippines is a country with beautiful scenery and culture. With scenic beaches that will make you wish you never had to leave, to ethnic people who have been around since the dawn of history. The Phillippines is a place where you can sit back and chill, or go out on an adventure and find something new. Here are some of the top destinations in the Phillippines.

Boracay

A very popular tourist destination that draws in tourists from all over the world; it is characterized by its beautiful beaches.

Tagaytay

Tagaytay is well known due to Taal Volcano, an inactive volcano that sits in the middle of a lake.

Puerto Princesa

well known for it's gorgeous underground river in Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park, it is a worth visit if you're looking for a bit of an adventure.
